## PhysicsPlayground

### Controls:

##### Keyboard:
	WASD - Move
	F - Unragdoll
	K - Toggle Kinematic
	UpArrow & DownArrow - Move Vehicle

##### Mouse:
	Left-click - Throw hammers
	Right-click drag - Rotate cameras
	Middle-mouse click - drag objects, holding [lShift] moves objects away
	from you, and holding [lCrtl] moves them towards you
